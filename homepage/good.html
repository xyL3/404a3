About Me

Hi, I'm Taylor Swift.

I'm an American singer-songwriter, and was born in Pennsylvania in 1989.

I started my career in country music when I was 14, and have released 9 studio albums, sold over 200 million records worldwide.

My Skills

I can write songs and the instruments I can play are guitars, piano, banjo and ukulele. As a singer, I have a mezzo-soprano vocal range.

I have collaborated with many different directors to produce my music videos.
